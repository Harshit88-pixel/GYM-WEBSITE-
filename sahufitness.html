Our Story

FROM A SINGLE BARBELL TO A MOVEMENT

It started with a small 400 sq ft training space in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, two power racks, and a refusal to build just another ordinary gym. Founder Harshit Sahu had spent years studying training methods and working with fitness enthusiasts, understanding exactly what serious training requires — and what was missing from most gyms in the city.

In 2012, he opened the first SAHU Fitness with one clear rule: no compromise on quality. The equipment would be premium. The training programs would be science-based. The trainers would be dedicated and knowledgeable — not just people with certificates. From the moment you walked in, the environment would push you to become better.

The results came fast. Within 18 months, memberships filled and demand kept growing. By 2015, SAHU Fitness expanded into a modern 8,000 sq ft facility in Raipur, built for strength training, performance, and transformation.

We haven't changed our standard. We've only raised it.

What Drives Us

OUR CORE VALUES

01
No Compromise

From the equipment we buy to the trainers we hire — we don't cut corners. Every decision is filtered through one question: is this the best possible option? If not, it doesn't make it through the door.

02
Science First

We follow peer-reviewed evidence, not fitness trends. Our programming is built on periodization science, biomechanics research, and sports psychology. We're not interested in what's popular. We're interested in what works.

03
Community Over Competition

The best training environments are the ones where everyone wants everyone else to succeed. We've built a culture where members celebrate each other's PRs, share knowledge, and push each other to show up even on the hard days.

04
Long Game Thinking

We're not building beach bodies for a single season. We're building athletes for life. Every program, every nutrition recommendation, every recovery protocol is designed for the kind of fitness that compounds over decades.

05
Accountability Culture

Results require consistency. Our trainers check in, our systems track progress, and our community holds each other accountable — not with judgment, but with genuine investment in each other's goals. Nobody falls through the cracks here.

06
Radical Inclusivity

Elite doesn't mean exclusive. Our programs serve everyone from first-timers to competitive athletes. The standard is the same for everyone — work hard, stay consistent, trust the process. What changes is the starting point, never the respect.

Common Questions

PROGRAMME FAQ

Can I try a class before committing to a membership? +
Yes. Your first group class at SAHU Fitness is completely free — no strings attached. Just book via the website or walk in. We want you to experience the quality before you decide.
I'm a complete beginner. Are any programmes suitable for me? +
Absolutely. Yoga & Mobility, Body Sculpt, and Boxing Fundamentals are specifically designed to meet beginners where they are. All new members also receive a complimentary fitness assessment on joining, so your trainer can match you to the right starting point.
Can I combine multiple programmes? +
Yes, and many of our members do. The most common combination is Strength & Power plus Yoga & Mobility, which is excellent for performance and recovery balance. Our trainers can help you build a weekly schedule that draws from multiple disciplines without overtraining.
How does class booking work? +
All classes are bookable through the SAHU Fitness app up to 7 days in advance. Elite and Apex members get priority booking, meaning they can reserve spots before Starter members. Classes typically fill 3–4 days out, so we recommend booking early.
What equipment is available for open training? +
The strength hall has 8 power racks, 4 Olympic platforms, a full cable tower system, chest-supported rows, leg press, hack squats, and an extensive free weights section from 2.5kg to 60kg dumbbells. The cardio deck has commercial treadmills, rowers, assault bikes, ski ergs, and echo bikes.

How We Coach

THE SAHU COACHING PHILOSOPHY

At SAHU, coaches don't just demonstrate and count reps. They diagnose, prescribe, and track. Every trainer operates from a coaching model built around three pillars: technical mastery, progressive programming, and honest communication.

We hire coaches who have competed in or deeply practised the disciplines they teach. We don't hire generalists who dabble. A powerlifting coach must have pulled serious weight. A boxing coach must have stood in front of an opponent. The depth of knowledge that comes from doing — not just studying — is irreplaceable.

"The best coaches make you better when they're not watching. That's the real standard."

Coaches at SAHU are also required to continue their own education — a minimum of 40 hours of CPD per year. The field evolves. Our coaches evolve with it. That commitment to staying current is what keeps our programming at the front of evidence-based practice.

PRICING FAQ

Is there a joining fee? +
There is no joining fee for the first 200 new members this month. After that, there's a one-time ₹500 joining fee that covers your fitness assessment, welcome pack, and app setup.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. All SAHU memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in. Give us 7 days notice before your billing date and you won't be charged for the following month. No cancellation fees, no awkward conversations.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date so you keep your current benefits for the rest of the month.
Do you offer student or corporate discounts? +
Yes. Students with valid ID get 20% off Starter and Elite plans. Corporate memberships (5+ employees from the same company) get a tiered discount of 15–30% depending on group size. Contact us for a corporate quote.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major credit and debit cards, and bank transfers. Auto-debit via mandate is available for hassle-free monthly billing.
